The saying is really "20% of the real estate agents carry out 80% of the business," and also this is true. The question is how could you find a really good real estate agent? The best real estate agent for you does not necessarily work at the largest brokerage, close the most transactions or make the most money. The best agent for you is a knowledgeable expert who will pay attention to you, ethically conduct herself and knows your market.Before setting your property on the market or setting out to purchase a new one, you need to identify real estate agents in your neighborhood who can assist with the sale. More than two million people nationwide have licenses to sell real estate, and it's their job to be an expert on the properties in their community. They track real estate trends and are in the business of helping others buy and sell homes. If you're in the market for a new home, it's wise to know how to find a real estate agent.The right agent can help you buy your dream house or sell your existing home quickly. The commission, paid from the sales proceeds, is actually usually divided equally between the listing agent and the selling broker.
